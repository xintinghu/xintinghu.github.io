Research Vision

My work connects real-world aviation operations, safety and risk modelling, AI-enabled optimization and learning, and stakeholder-oriented system development.

I translate operational challenges in low-altitude mobility and air traffic management into rigorous methods and research prototypes that support safe, resilient, and practical decision-making.
